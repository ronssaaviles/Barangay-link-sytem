DOCUMENT REQUEST

Certificate of Residency

This document certifies that you are a legitimate resident of the barangay. It is commonly required for:

  • School enrollment and requirements
  • Employment requirements
  • Proof of address for various transactions

Processing Time: 1-2 business days

Barangay Clearance

A document certifying that you have no derogatory record in the barangay. This is required for:

  • Job applications
  • Business permit applications
  • Police clearance requirements

Processing Time: 1-2 business days

Certificate of Indigency

This document certifies that you are from a low-income family and may need financial assistance. Used for:

  • Medical assistance
  • Educational assistance
  • Other social services

Processing Time: 1-2 business days
